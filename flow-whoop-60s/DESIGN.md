# Flow WHOOP 60s Design

## Style Prompt

A vertical 4K CrossFit sports film with a professional performance-tech tone. The image should feel high-contrast, controlled, and physical: deep shadows, natural skin, green WHOOP data accents, red/orange impact flashes, fine grain, and precise HUD overlays that never dominate the athlete.

## Colors

- `#050706` deep black canvas and letter-free edges
- `#f4f1e8` warm white text and highlight lines
- `#c7ff00` WHOOP performance green and sensor data
- `#ff3b1f` impact red for transitions and strain emphasis
- `#16d7ff` cool cyan for technical grid details

## Typography

- Display: `Arial Black`, `Impact`, system sans-serif
- Data: `SFMono-Regular`, `Menlo`, `Consolas`, monospace

## Motion Rules

- Cut fast in the first three seconds, then alternate hard impacts with short breathing beats.
- Use push-in, shake, scan, flash, and glitch accents only around effort points.
- Keep data compact on the right side or lower edge, never over the athlete's torso during key movement.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use soft pastel palettes, decorative blobs, or generic tech gradients.
- Do not cover the athlete with a large data card.
- Do not make the video feel like a static fitness log.
- Do not overuse text; every word should feel like a punch or data signal.
