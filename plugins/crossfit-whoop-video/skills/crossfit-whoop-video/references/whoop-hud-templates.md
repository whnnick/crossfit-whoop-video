# WHOOP HUD Templates

Use `assets/whoop-hud-templates.json` for the machine-readable catalog.

## Preferred Combination

Use these three styles for a cinematic sports ad:

- `broadcast_bright`: opening context, closest to the darker May 19 reference but brighter.
- `glass_side_hud`: middle intensity data, right-side translucent panel with large heart-rate number.
- `summary_card`: ending summary, large bottom card with multiple metrics.

## Additional Styles

- `compact_telemetry`: small corner card when action must stay unobstructed.
- `redline_peak`: 1-2 second alert for maximum intensity.
- `minimal_ticker`: bottom strip when only lightweight data is needed.

## Motion Patterns

Use subtle motion:

- number roll/count-up
- progress bar growth
- scan-line sweep
- panel slide-in/out
- short beat flash
- final glow on summary card

Do not make overlays animate constantly through the whole video.
